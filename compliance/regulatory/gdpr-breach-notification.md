## <a name="what-constitute-a-breach-of-personal-data-under-the-gdpr"></a><span data-ttu-id="85eaf-108">根据 GDPR，什么构成了个人数据泄露行为？</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="85eaf-108">What constitute a breach of personal data under the GDPR?</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="85eaf-109">个人数据是指，所有与个人相关、可直接或间接表明身份的信息。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="85eaf-109">Personal data means any information related to an individual that can be used to identify them directly or indirectly.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="85eaf-110">个人数据泄露是指，“导致传输、存储或以其他方式处理的个人数据遭到意外或非法破坏、丢失、更改、未经授权披露或访问的违反安全之事宜。”</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="85eaf-110">A personal data breach is 'a breach of security leading to the accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorized disclosure of, or access to, personal data transmitted, stored, or otherwise processed'.</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="85eaf-113">*数据控制者（控制者）*：单独或与他人共同确定个人数据处理目的和方法的法人、公共机关、机构或其他团体。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="85eaf-113">*Data Controller (Controller)*: A legal person, public authority, agency or other body which, alone or jointly with others, determines the purposes and means of the processing of personal data.</span></span>
