## <a name="ccpa-overview"></a><span data-ttu-id="6eed2-105">CCPA 概述</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6eed2-105">CCPA overview</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="6eed2-106">加州消费者隐私法案 (CCPA) 美国第一个全面的隐私法。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6eed2-106">The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) is the first comprehensive privacy law in the United States.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6eed2-107">它向加州消费者提供各种隐私权利。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6eed2-107">It provides a variety of privacy rights to California consumers.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="6eed2-108">CCPA 监管的企业会向这些消费者履行多项义务，包括披露、一般数据保护条例 (GDPR) 等消费者数据主体权利 (DSR) 、某些数据传输的"选择退出"和未成年人的"选择加入"要求。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6eed2-108">Businesses regulated by the CCPA will have a number of obligations to those consumers, including disclosures, General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)-like consumer data subject rights (DSRs), an 'opt-out' for certain data transfers, and an 'opt-in' requirement for minors.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="6eed2-109">CCPA 仅适用于在加利福尼亚经营业务但满足以下一项或一项以上条件的公司： (1) 年收入超过 2，500 万美元， 或 (2) 通过销售加利福尼亚消费者个人信息获得其年收入超过 50%，或者每年购买、销售或共享超过 50，000 个加州消费者的个人信息的 (3) 。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6eed2-109">The CCPA only applies to companies doing business in California which satisfy one or more of the following: (1) have a gross annual revenue of more than $25 million, or (2) derive more than 50% of their annual income from the sale of California consumer personal information, or (3) buy, sell or share the personal information of more than 50,000 California consumers annually.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="6eed2-110">CCPA 将于 2020 年 1 月 1 日生效。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6eed2-110">The CCPA goes into effect on January 1, 2020.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6eed2-111">但是，由加州律师总 (AG) 将于 2020 年 7 月 1 日开始。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6eed2-111">However, enforcement by the California Attorney General (AG) will start on July 1, 2020.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="6eed2-112">加州公司将强制执行 CCPA，并拥有对不符合规定罚款的罚款。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6eed2-112">The California AG will enforce the CCPA and will have power to issue non-compliance fines.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6eed2-113">CCPA 还提供了仅限于数据泄露的私有行动权利。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6eed2-113">The CCPA also provides a private right of action which is limited to data breaches.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6eed2-114">私人诉权规定，每位消费者在每个事件中受到的损失可能介于 100 美元到 750 美元之间。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6eed2-114">Under the private right of action, damages can come in between $100 and $750 per incident per consumer.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6eed2-115">加州司法部长还可在整体上实施 CCPA，可就每次违规征收不超过 2,500 美元的民事罚款，就每次故意违规征收 7,500 的的民事罚款。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6eed2-115">The California AG also can enforce the CCPA in its entirety with the ability to levy a civil penalty of not more than $2,500 per violation or $7,500 per intentional violation.</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="6eed2-151">在隐私策略中提供有关所收集的个人信息的来源、业务目的和类别的更详细的披露，包括如何将这些类别出售或转移到其他实体。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6eed2-151">Provide more detailed disclosures in a privacy policy regarding the sources, business purposes, and categories of personal information that is collected, including how those categories are sold or transferred to other entities.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="6eed2-157">**CCPA 如何适用于儿童？**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6eed2-157">**How does the CCPA apply to children?**</span></span>
