<span data-ttu-id="78482-106">《健康保险可移植性和责任法案》 (HIPAA) 是一项美国医疗保健法律，它规定使用、披露和维护个人身份健康信息的要求。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="78482-106">The Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) is a US healthcare law that establishes requirements for the use, disclosure, and safeguarding of individually identifiable health information.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="78482-107">它适用于覆盖的实体、医院办公室、医院、医疗保健机构和其他医疗保健公司，可以访问患者受保护的健康信息 (PHI) ，以及代表其处理 PHI 的业务关联（如云服务和 IT 提供商）。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="78482-107">It applies to covered entities, doctors' offices, hospitals, health insurers, and other healthcare companies, with access to patients' protected health information (PHI), as well as to business associates, such as cloud service and IT providers, that process PHI on their behalf.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="78482-108"> (大多数涵盖的实体不自行执行声明或数据处理等功能;他们依赖业务关联来这样做。) </span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="78482-108">(Most covered entities do not carry out functions such as claims or data processing on their own; they rely on business associates to do so.)</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="78482-109">法律在四个一般领域规范 PHI 的使用和传播：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="78482-109">The law regulates the use and dissemination of PHI in four general areas:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="78482-112">标识符，即出于研究目的收集时无法释放的信息类型。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="78482-112">Identifiers, which are the types of information that cannot be released if collected for research purposes.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="78482-113">与医疗保健相关的交易（包括资格、保险索赔和付款）中数据的电子传输代码。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="78482-113">Codes for electronic transmission of data in healthcare-related transactions, including eligibility and insurance claims and payments.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="78482-114">HIPAA 的范围通过《针对经济与健康 (HITECH) 法案》的制定而扩展。HIPAA 和 HITECH 法案规则包括：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="78482-114">The scope of HIPAA was extended with the enactment of the Health Information Technology for Economic and Clinical Health (HITECH) Act. - <span data-ttu-id="78482-115">HIPAA 隐私规则侧重于个人控制其个人信息使用的权利，并涵盖 PHI 的机密性，限制其使用和披露。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="78482-115">The HIPAA Privacy Rule, which focuses on the right of individuals to control the use of their personal information, and covers the confidentiality of PHI, limiting its use and disclosure.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="78482-116">HIPAA 安全规则，它设置了管理、技术和物理安全措施标准，以保护电子 PHI 免受未经授权的访问、使用和泄露。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="78482-116">The HIPAA Security Rule, which sets the standards for administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect electronic PHI from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="78482-117">它还包括业务关联协议和 (等组织) 。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="78482-117">It also includes such organizational requirements as Business Associate Agreements (BAAs).</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="78482-118">HITECH 泄露通知最终规则，要求在出现不安全 PHI 泄露时通知个人和政府。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="78482-118">The HITECH Breach Notification Final Rule, which requires giving notice to individuals and the government when a breach of unsecured PHI occurs.</span></span>
