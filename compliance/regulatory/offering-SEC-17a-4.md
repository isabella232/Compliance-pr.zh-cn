## <a name="about-sec-rule-17a-4f"></a><span data-ttu-id="2795a-105">关于 SEC 规则 17a-4 (f) </span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2795a-105">About SEC Rule 17a-4(f)</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="2795a-106">美国[证券交易Exchange委员会 (SEC) ](https://www.sec.gov/)是美国政府的独立机构，是美国证券交易委员会的主要监管机构和监管机构。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2795a-106">The [US Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC)](https://www.sec.gov/) is an independent agency of the US federal government and the primary overseer and regulator of US securities markets.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2795a-107">它行使对联邦证券法的强制执行权限，提出新的证券规则，并监督证券行业的市场监管。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2795a-107">It wields enforcement authority over federal securities laws, proposes new securities rules, and oversees market regulation of the securities industry.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="2795a-108">SEC 对选择在电子存储媒体上保留书籍和记录的受管制实体定义了严格和明确的要求。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2795a-108">The SEC defines rigorous and explicit requirements for regulated entities that elect to retain books and records on electronic storage media.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2795a-109">它建立了 [17 CFR 240.17a-3](https://www.govinfo.gov/app/details/CFR-2012-title17-vol3/CFR-2012-title17-vol3-sec240-17a-3) 和 [17 CFR 240.17a-4，](https://www.ecfr.gov/cgi-bin/text-idx?mc=true&node=pt17.4.240&rgn=div5#se17.4.240_117a_64) 以监管证券经纪人的记录保管（包括保留期）。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2795a-109">It established [17 CFR 240.17a-3](https://www.govinfo.gov/app/details/CFR-2012-title17-vol3/CFR-2012-title17-vol3-sec240-17a-3) and [17 CFR 240.17a-4](https://www.ecfr.gov/cgi-bin/text-idx?mc=true&node=pt17.4.240&rgn=div5#se17.4.240_117a_64) to regulate recordkeeping, including retention periods, for securities broker-dealers.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2795a-110">随后 [，SEC](https://www.sec.gov/rules/interp/34-47806.htm) 修正了 17 CFR 240.17a-4 段落 (f) ，明确发布两个解释性版本，以允许书籍和记录在电子存储媒体上保留，只要满足某些条件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2795a-110">Later, the SEC [amended](https://www.sec.gov/rules/interp/34-47806.htm) 17 CFR 240.17a-4 paragraph (f), issuing two interpretive releases expressly to allow books and records to be retained on electronic storage media as long as certain conditions were met.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="2795a-111">如果电子存储系统在所需的保留期内阻止更改或清除记录，它将满足这些条件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2795a-111">An electronic storage system meets those conditions if it prevents the alteration or erasure of records for the required retention period.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2795a-112">根据记录类型，保留期从 3 到 6 年，前两年要求立即访问。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2795a-112">Retention periods vary from three to six years based on record types, with immediate accessibility mandated for the first two years.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2795a-113">此外，其中一个解释性版本要求存储系统能够保留超过 SEC 建立的保留期的记录，以遵守副发、法定保留或其他此类要求。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2795a-113">Moreover, one of the interpretive releases requires that the storage system be capable of retaining records beyond the SEC-established retention period to comply with subpoenas, legal hold, or other such requirements.</span></span>
