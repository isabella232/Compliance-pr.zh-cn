<span data-ttu-id="68f56-105">根据一般数据保护条例 (GDPR)，数据控制者需要为“可能对自然人的权限和自由造成高风险”的处理操作准备数据保护影响评估 (DPIA)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="68f56-105">Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), data controllers are required to prepare a Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) for processing operations that are 'likely to result in a high risk to the rights and freedoms of natural persons'.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="68f56-106">Microsoft Office 365 本身没有固有的内容，这必然要求使用它的数据控制者创建 DPIA。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="68f56-106">There is nothing inherent in Microsoft Office 365 that would necessarily require the creation of a DPIA by a data controller using it.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="68f56-107">无论是否需要 DPIA，都将依赖于你（数据控制者）部署、配置和使用 Office 365 的详细信息和上下文。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="68f56-107">Rather, whether a DPIA is required will be dependent on the details and context of how you, as the data controller, deploy, configure, and use Office 365.</span></span>
